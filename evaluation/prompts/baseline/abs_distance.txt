## **Distance Estimation Task**

Goal: Calculate the distance between two specific objects from the closest points (e.g., "table and dishwasher").
---

Step 1: Identify the two objects
  Extract from question:
    - Object A: (e.g., "table")
    - Object B: (e.g., "dishwasher")
  NEVER stop at the first match. There might be multiple objects of the same category.

Step 2: Video observation
  ✅ Single numerical value (e.g., "2.5m") ❌ No ranges (e.g., "2-3m")
  ```
  Video: [Object A] is at [location]. [Object B] is at [location].
  Visual relationship: [description]
  Estimated distance: ~X.Xm
  ```
